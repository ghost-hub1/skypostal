Learn More

Close Modal

The Brazilian eCommerce market is a dynamic and rapidly expanding opportunity that sellers cannot afford to overlook. Brazil’s eager digital shoppers can be found on major eCommerce sites like Amazon. 

To enter this market and leverage our partnership, it’s necessary to understand the basics of  Brazil Programa Remessa Conforme (PRC), which was designed by the Brazilian government to simplify the import process into Brazil.

  • Reduced import duties: Lower import duties and taxes compared to traditional import procedures 
  • Simplified Documentation: Less documentation is required, streamlining the import process and reducing the administrative burden on US sellers
  • Faster Customs Clearance: Faster clearance means quicker delivery of goods to Brazilian customers

What do these benefits mean for sellers? Lower costs, less documentation, and faster transit times will strengthen the presence of US sellers within the Brazilian market.
