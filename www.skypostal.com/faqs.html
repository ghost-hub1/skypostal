Merchant FAQ

SkyPostal is committed to anticipating your needs and providing you with answers to our most Frequently Asked Questions.

Technology

What do we need to get started?

Shipping with SkyPostal is simple! To get started, submit a sales inquiry.

How quick is the integration time?

Integrating with SkyPostal can take minutes. Our exclusive integrations include Easyship, Shopify, Shipstation, and Direct API. 

Many of our integrations are free. We also offer complimentary onboarding support. For integration specific questions, please send us an email.

Custom and Duties

How are duties calculated for international orders?

Duty and tax calculations vary per country. For more information, download our Service Guide.

Do you handle duties payment?

We can collect duties payment in advance from all of our countries via our partnership with SecureImport. Through this exclusive service, consignees will be contacted via email prior to shipment of their items, requesting payment in advance for applicable duties. Packages are not shipped until duties have been collected. Duties payment requests to consignees include the Name of the Store where the items were purchased, the Order Description and Order Amount.

How does customs clearance work?

SkyPostal handles every detail associated with customs, so you don’t have to. We will prepare documentation to clear customs and pay duties on behalf of our clients. Payment for duties can be collected prior to shipping from our clients (DDP) or through the consignees directly (Duty Collect). If you have any questions, please visit our contact page.
